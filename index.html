Lebanon Township Volunteer Fire Department

About Us

We provide Fire Protection and Emergency Medical Service (EMS) to our community, as well as mutual aid services to our surrounding municipalities. All of our services are performed by your friends and neighbors, our volunteers, and are performed with professionalism and excellence, free of charge to the recipient.
